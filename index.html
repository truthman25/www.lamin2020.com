Love Story

Love Story

  • July 2016

    First We Met

    It was around July, 2016 at the University of Jos. Having many things in common especially in the aspect of the deen. Our relationship was ignited with a vision of building a home upon the love and consciousness of Allah.

  • June, 2017

    Our Relationship

    It has been one full of mutual support and understanding. Though we had our moments of highs and lows; with prayers, we built the confidence that our vision would lead us to a great destination.

  • June, 2018

    Growing together in Deen

    Upon getting together, in a bid to grow in the deen and influence lives positively we started TheDeenTalkTV on Whatsapp and facebook and Alhamdulillah a lot has been achieved.

  • June, 2020

    We're getting married

    Our families met in 2019, projections and decisions were made and here we are; getting married to the fulfilment of our vision. (Insha Allah)!
